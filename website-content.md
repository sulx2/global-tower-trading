## 1. Company Overview

GLOBAL TOWER FOR TRADING is an international trading company supplying hardware,
industrial, and construction materials across the UAE and Oman markets. With a
sourcing office in Anping, Hebei — China's hardware and wire-mesh manufacturing
hub — and operations in Oman, the company connects reliable global manufacturing
with regional industrial demand.

The business covers import, export, equipment rental, and hardware supply,
delivering a broad range of construction-grade products through a single trusted
supply partner.

## 2. Core Business

- **Import** — Sourcing quality hardware and industrial goods from trusted global manufacturers.
- **Export** — Supplying construction and industrial materials to regional and international markets.
- **Equipment Rental** — Industrial lifting, material-handling, and site equipment for project needs.
- **Hardware Supply** — Wholesale and project supply of hardware, fixings, mesh, steel, and tools.
