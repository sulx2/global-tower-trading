Do not copy them exactly.
Analyze their premium industrial style, hero sections, animations, typography, spacing, service layout, and contact sections.

Website Goal:
Create a unique premium industrial website for GLOBAL TOWER FOR TRADING.

The website should feel:
Expensive, powerful, corporate, industrial, modern, and suitable for UAE/Oman market.

Important features to include:

* Premium hero section
* Dark overlay over industrial images
* Sticky navbar
* Smooth scroll animations
* Parallax image movement
* Animated statistics
* Product category cards
* Services section
* Why Choose Us section
* Contact section
* Floating WhatsApp button
* Fully responsive mobile design

Important:
Do not make the website look like a simple catalog.
Use the brochure only to understand the company, services, and product categories.
